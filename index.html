LoanMe

What do we do?

Here at LoanMe, we link you with different loaning companies that can provide you with low-interest loans. After passing the customers' details through our machine learning algorithm, which has above 90% accuracy, we are able to link selected loan applicants with low-interest loan companies.

How to go about it

- Sign up on our site on the Sign Up page.
- Apply for a loan on the Application Page. Our algorithm provides a full report on your loan status.
- Check your loan status on the Results Page after completing your application.
