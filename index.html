Welcome to LoanME

What do we do?

Here at LoanME, we link you with different loaning companies that can provide you with low-interest loans. After passing the customers details through our machine learning algorithm, which has above 90% accuracy. With our almost perfect algorithm, we are able to link selected loan applicants with low-interest loan companies.

How to go about it.

- Visit the Sign Up Page to sign up into our site. - Apply for a loan under the application page. This application gives a full report on your loan status after passing it through the Machine learning algorithm. You can do this on the Application Page. - Get a full report on the status of your loan. You can get this on the Results Page. You would get redirect immediately after you complete your application.
